About Me

Ground-Level Hustle.
Strategic Thinking.

I spent nearly 4 years at PhonePe building merchant networks from the ground up — 250+ partnerships, territory growth, and a lot of cold calls that turned into long-term relationships. I learned that BD isn't about scripts; it's about understanding what the other person actually needs.

Now at Bytecode Agency, I'm applying the same ground-level hustle to IT/SaaS — connecting founders who need development partners with an agency that can deliver. It's early days here, and I'm building the pipeline intentionally rather than fast.

What sets me apart is simple: most BD folks can't assess tech talent, and most recruiters can't sell. I do both — which means I'm useful on both sides of a deal.

Nearly 4 years in field sales taught me one thing: relationships compound. The merchant you help properly today sends you three referrals next year.
